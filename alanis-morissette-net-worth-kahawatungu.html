Alanis Morissette’s meteoric rise to fame came with the release of her breakthrough album, “Jagged Little Pill,” in 1995. This iconic record propelled Morissette to international stardom, with its raw, confessional lyrics and infectious melodies resonating deeply with listeners. Tracks like “You Oughta Know” catapulted Morissette to the forefront of the alternative rock scene, earning her critical acclaim and commercial success.
