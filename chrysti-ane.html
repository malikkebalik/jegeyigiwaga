Chrysti Ane- Biography

Chrystiane Lopes, often known as Chrysti Ane, is a Brazilian model and actress. She is most recognized for her role as Sarah Thompson, the Pink Ranger, in the American television series ‘Power Rangers Ninja Steel.’ Ane began her modeling career as a fitness model since she was passionate about exercise. Following a series of viral Instagram postings, she joined with the American startup Fitonomy to make fitness videos, bolstering her image as a fitness lover. At the same time, she pursued an acting career, appearing in films such as ‘Malhacao’ and ‘A Housekeeper’s Revenge.’ Following these minor appearances, Ane had her big break when she was cast as the Pink Ranger in the hit series ‘Power Rangers Ninja Steel.’ This not only enhanced her visibility in the profession but also her fame in the media. She recently made headlines when it was revealed that she was pregnant and had a relationship with actor Ryan Guzman. Ane is a successful entrepreneur in addition to her performing career. She co-owns a luxury clothes business and has her own fitness guide website. She is currently residing in Los Angeles.

Chrystiane Lopes was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, on July 26, 1993. Her family relocated to the United States when she was eight years old, despite the fact that she spent her formative years in Brazil. She participated in a variety of sports as a child, but she was most passionate about dancing and thus chose to pursue a career in entertainment. She is currently engaged to Ryan Guzman, an actor. Ane chose Instagram to announce her impending pregnancy with a newborn boy. Her interests include working out at the gym and dancing. She is also an adrenaline junkie who enjoys extreme hobbies such as skiing, roller coasters, and motorbike road trips.

Chrysti Ane- Professional Career

Chrysti Ane began her acting career by studying at Los Angeles’ famed Speiser/Sturgess Acting Studio. Following this, she made her film debut in 2013 with ‘Malhacao.’ In 2014, she appeared in a brief role as Kayla in the TV movie ‘A Housekeeper’s Revenge.’ At the same time, Ane gradually gained notoriety for her Instagram photos focusing on fitness and workouts. Her social media following grew significantly when she became a fitness model. Soon after, she collaborated with the US-based fitness startup Fitonomy and starred in a series of training videos. When she saw that many of her followers were looking for workout suggestions and assistance, she launched her own YouTube channel to publish workout vlogs. There are currently over 15,000 subscribers to this channel. Ane broadened her understanding of fitness by launching her own website, which was later followed by the creation of an app. The website provides training recommendations for both men and women, and the app allows customers to monitor their efforts.
Ane’s breakthrough came in 2017 when she was cast as the Pink Ranger, Sarah Thompson, in the American TV series ‘Power Rangers Ninja Steel.’ Ane regards this as her most crucial job after putting in a lot of effort throughout the auditions. Ane has a business side to her in addition to acting and fitness modeling. She is a co-owner of the Luxmafia luxury clothing boutique. She is also a well-known social media influencer, with over 350k Instagram followers and over 29k Twitter followers.
