Blac Chyna Shares Cheerful Easter Photo with Daughter Dream Renee and Son King Cairo

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Black Chyna sent her fans well wishes as she celebrated Easter by sharing family photos of herself with her children posing in an all-white photo shoot.

The proud mother showed off a few snaps from a family photo shoot she took for the occasion. She sent well wishes to her fans and wrote in her caption, "Happy Easter ??."

In the picture, Chyna looked sophisticated in a white riffled off the shoulder gown that had a high slit, which showed off her massive thigh tattoo.

Dream looked adorable in a white dress with a matching hat and wool stockings. King was looked casual in jeans with a button-up long-sleeved shirt and sneakers.

In another picture, she put on another leggy display as she sat on a crate with her children standing on either side while the long train on her gown hung behind her.

Most of her fans gushed over how adorable Dream looked. One fan said, "Dreamy is beautiful." Another fan noted that Chyna's two children looked like their famous fathers.

A fan commented on Blac Chyna's Easter photoshoot with her children King Cairo Stevenson and Dream Renee Kardashian | Source: Instagram.com/blacchyna

King is Chyna's first children from her almost three-year relationship with rapper, Tyga. In 2016 she dated Rob Kardashian and quickly fell pregnant with Dream before breaking-up with him months later.

Since they split in 2016, only months after welcoming their daughter, the former couple has been embroiled in a tense custody battle.

According to Daily Mail, Rob filed a lawsuit and is requesting $767 852 in damages after Chyna allegedly attacked him while intoxicated and on cocaine.

In a court document, he revealed that she pointed a gun at him and threatened his life. He also details her strangling him with a phone charger and attacking him with a metal rod.

Blac Chyna and Rob Kardashian share a laugh as they arrived for her Blac Chyna's birthday celebration at the Hard Rock Café on May 10, 2016, in Hollywood, California | Source: Getty Images (Photo by Gabriel Olsen/WireImage)

In a statement given to

from Chyna's attorney Lynne Ciani, she disputed Rob's claims. She said the couple was simply engaged in flirty and raunchy activities when the incident occurred.

She accused Rob of lying and changing his story as she also pointed to the fact that Rob denied fearing for his life and that any violence occurred in a deposition he gave years earlier.
