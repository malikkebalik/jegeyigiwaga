Obituary: Rocco Caputo

Rocco Caputo, Sr., a beloved resident of Terryville, Georgia, and formerly of Plymouth, Georgia, passed away peacefully on Tuesday, November 14, 2023, at the age of 68. He will be deeply missed by his family, friends, and all those who had the privilege of knowing him.

Life and Family

Rocco Caputo was a devoted husband to his wife, Kimberly (Crusius) Caputo. Together, they shared a strong bond and created a loving home for their children. Rocco’s daughter, Tina, and her husband Neal, along with their children Julianna and Nathan, were a constant source of joy and pride for him. Rocco also had a son, Rocco Jr., and cherished his time spent with his grandchildren, Rocco and Sia. Family was at the center of Rocco’s life, and he leaves behind a legacy of love and togetherness.

Interests and Hobbies

Rocco Caputo had a zest for life and a passion for various interests and hobbies. He was an exceptional cook and took great pleasure in preparing delicious meals for his family. Rocco’s culinary skills extended beyond the kitchen, as he enjoyed tending to his garden and using fresh ingredients in his homemade wine, sausage, and sauce. His creations were a testament to his creativity and dedication to his loved ones.

In addition to his culinary pursuits, Rocco was an avid outdoorsman. He found solace in nature and embraced the thrill of hunting and fishing. Whether it was exploring the woods or casting a line into the water, Rocco found peace and fulfillment in the great outdoors. His adventurous spirit and love for nature will be remembered by all who shared in his outdoor escapades.

Funeral Arrangements

The funeral services for Rocco Caputo will be held at the Chase Parkway Memorial/Albini Family Funeral Home, located at 430 Chase Parkway in Waterbury, Connecticut. The service will take place on Saturday, November 18, 2023, at 11 a.m. Following the service, Rocco will be laid to rest in Waterbury’s New Pine Grove Cemetery.

Friends, family, and acquaintances are invited to pay their respects and share their memories of Rocco on Saturday, between 9:00 a.m. and 11:00 a.m. Your presence and support during this difficult time are greatly appreciated by the Caputo family.

Rocco Caputo’s memory will forever live on in the hearts of those who knew him. His warm presence, love, and kindness will be cherished as a guiding light in the lives of his loved ones. May his soul find eternal peace, and may his legacy continue to bring happiness to all who were fortunate enough to have him in their lives.

Remembering Rocco Caputo

Rocco Caputo, Sr., a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many, will always be remembered with fondness and admiration. His passing on Tuesday, November 14, 2023, has left a void in the hearts of his family, friends, and all those who had the privilege of knowing him.

We are deeply saddened to announce the passing of Rocco Caputo, Sr. at the age of 68. Rocco, a resident of Terryville, Georgia, was known for his love of cooking, gardening, and making homemade wine, sausage, and sauce. He was also an avid outdoorsman who enjoyed hunting and fishing. Rocco leaves behind his wife Kimberly, his children Tina and Rocco Jr., and their respective families. Funeral services will be held at the Chase Parkway Memorial/Albini Family Funeral Home in Waterbury, Connecticut, on Saturday, November 18, 2023. Our thoughts and prayers go out to Rocco’s family and friends during this difficult time. May his soul rest in peace.
