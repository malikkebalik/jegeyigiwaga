    Vicky Kaya Facts

  • She started her career as a model at the age of 16.
  • She was first approached for modeling in a street.
  • Vicky migrated to Paris at the age of 18 to pursue modeling and stayed there for 4 years. She also relocated to Milan, London, and Germany for work.
  • She has been featured in campaigns of the famous cosmetic products such as Johnson and Johnson, L’Oreal Paris, Dove, Virginia Slims, Garnier Paris, Nivea, and Schwarzkopf.
  • In Greece, she has been the main campaign spokesperson for Nivea, Lancia, and Vodafone.
  • She has received the ‘Model of the Year 2005’ award at the Life & Style Women of the Year Awards held in 2006.
  • Vicky also lent her voice to the character of Sally in the Greek version of the famous Pixar/Disney movie Cars.
  • In 2007 Women of the Year Life & Style Awards, she received the “Best Newcomer” award for her lead role in The Producers.
  • Vicky has hosted shows like So You Think You Can Dance and Next Top Model Greek version. Also, she has been a host for Wanna Bet (2008), Morning Coffee (2009), Miss Greece Beauty Pageant (2009, 2010), and Madwalk by Vodafone (2011).
  • She is also the founder of a non-profitable charity group called ‘Vicky and Friends’, that works with other small charity groups for fundraising.
