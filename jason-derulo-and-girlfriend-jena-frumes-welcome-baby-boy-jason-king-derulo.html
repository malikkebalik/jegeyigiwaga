Jason Derulo and Girlfriend Jena Frumes Welcome Baby Boy Jason King Derulo

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Jason Derulo and his girlfriend Jena Frumes are officially parents! The two welcomed their baby boy and shared adorable photos of their little king in a sweet Instagram post on May 19, 2021.

The couple was blessed with their first child together on May 8, 2021, and announced it after their first week as happy parents. Frumes took to Instagram to share a few portraits of their son that were taken at the hospital after she gave birth.

Giving fans their first look at him, Frumes penned a heartfelt message that read, “Life now has so much more meaning & I am so grateful. I’m so so so in love with this little boy he’s everything I never knew I needed."

Derulo also posted a professional reel documenting their journey to parenthood on his Instagram on Tuesday night. 

The Lifestyle singer described the day as the happiest day of his life as he brought his little boy, Jason King Derulo, home. “He’s so lucky to have such a strong caring hero of a mother,” he added.

The pair was careful not to reveal the baby's face, but they were thrilled to share the good news with their fans. 

The Savage Love artist initially shared the pregnancy news in March 2021 alongside a cute beachfront video. Derulo noted he couldn’t be more excited about the new chapter of their lives.

The expectant mother also shared a picture of Derulo cradling her with a baby bump and simply captioned it “Mom & Dad.”

Days later, the pair confirmed their baby's gender by shooting off blue fireworks to reveal they were expecting a son. The intimate party took place at the Privilege Pool at Baha Mar resort destination in the Bahamas with a group of close friends and family.
