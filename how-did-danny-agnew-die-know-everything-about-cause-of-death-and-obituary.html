Who was Danny Agnew?

Danny Agnew was a widely respected figure in Liberty City, known not only for his business acumen but for his unwavering commitment to racial equity and social justice. Along with his wife, Agnew founded The Roots Collective in 2015, a business that specialized in selling Black empowerment apparel and provided a space for like-minded businesses in the Roots Black House.

Agnew was a driving force in bringing economic opportunities to the Black community in Liberty City. He believed that Black businesses were central to building sustainable wealth and building a better future for the community. His tireless advocacy work and leadership in the Black Lives Matter movement gained him national recognition and respect.

How did Danny Agnew die? What was the cause of his death?

On june 15, 2023, Danny Agnew lost his life due to a horrific car accident on Interstate 95. Reports indicate that eight cars were involved in the accident, which occurred at around 2:10 a.m.

While the details of the circumstances surrounding the accident are still unclear, what we do know is that Agnew was driving a Tesla that crashed into a concrete barrier before coming to a stop in the middle of the highway. Tragically, a Dodge sedan then collided with the Tesla, and the driver was ejected from the vehicle. A white Nissan was unable to avoid the accident and collided with a Chevrolet SUV, resulting in the Nissan running over Agnew. Despite several minor crashes involving other vehicles, it was Agnew who tragically lost his life from injuries sustained in the fatal crash.
