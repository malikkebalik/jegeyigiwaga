How Old is Sofia Carson?

Sofia was born on April 10, 1993, in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and is now 30 years old. She was raised in a family of 4, including her 2 parents and a younger sister.

Carson went to Miami’s Carrollton School of the Sacred Heart and St Hugh School. She is also known to be an alumnus of the University of Califonia, Los Angeles.

Sofia later enrolled at the Motion Dance Studio, where she was part of the IMPAC Youth Ensemble program, competing across the United States. She subsequently attended UCLA, majoring in communications with a minor in French. 

Who are the Actress’ Parents?

Sofia Carson’s parents are José F. Daccarett and Laura Char Carson. The former is her father while the latter is her mother. Her mother is famous for being part of the Char family, which exerts significant influence in Barranquilla’s business and political sectors. Her parents met and married each other in Florida.

Her Siblings: Who are they?

Paulina Char is Sofia’s only sibling. Paulina is a professional in the beauty sector. She is also an executive at Tower 28 Beauty, where she contributes to the development of cosmetics.

The celebrity sister was born on July 25, 1995, meaning she is 28 years old at the moment. She previously explained that she always had a deep love for beauty products and makeup since she was a little girl and used to go through her mom’s makeup stash to experiment.

Just a glance at Sofia and Paulina’s will reveal to you how close they are. They frequently share photos of one another, and Paulina frequently attends red-carpet events with her Sofia.

More so, the sisters have also acknowledged that despite their many differences, they are able to balance and complete one another flawlessly. They also think that deep down, they have a lot of similar principles and ideals that were passed down to them by their family, especially their mother.
