Tamron Hall Is Dating: Former MSNBC Personality Is Open To Having Children

Many people may not recall, but rumors about Lawrence and Tamron Hall’s romance circulated long before the former’s divorce was finalized.

In February of 2012, the New York Post was the first media outlet to report on the developing romance between the MSNBC personalities.
Neither Lawrence nor his alleged girlfriend, who has a sizable salary and net worth, have publicly addressed the rumors.

After attending the 2016 White House Correspondents’ Dinner, the rumored couple sparked dating rumors once more. Tamron revealed her plans in an interview with People Magazine earlier that year, adding fuel to the fire.

“I haven’t given up hope of having a child.
But I hope that whatever path I take to parenthood, whether it’s adoption or I’m able to conceive, I’m able to give someone as beautiful a life as my parents gave me.”

Unfortunately, Tamron revealed on Twitter in 2017 that she was dating a man named Tony. She left MSBNC and the Today Show less than a month later. She is now happily married to music manager Steven Greener. Tamron’s lifelong dream was realized when the couple welcomed their first child, Mosses, in April 2019; the same year they married.

The former Today Show co-host shared the joyous news on Instagram, posting a photo of herself holding her bundle of joy. She was wearing a homemade hat with the word Moses written across it in the photo.

Tamron revealed the news two months after she opened up about her marriage on Instagram. She revealed her pregnancy to her followers in the same video.

Lawrence has been preoccupied with politics while his alleged ex-girlfriend has been preoccupied with parenting. In May 2019, the polarizing anchor sparked controversy by accusing White House Counselor Kellyanne Conway of violating the Hatch Act.
His claims came after Kellyanne allegedly made disparaging remarks about presidential candidate Joe Biden.
