Chloe Grace Moretz Relationship History, Dating

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Chloe Grace Moretz Relationship History, Dating – Chloë Grace Moretz, the star of movies like Kick-Ass and The Equalizer, made headlines when she confirmed her relationship with Brooklyn Beckham, son of Victoria and David Beckham. In this article, we’ll delve into their journey, from dating speculations to public appearances, and explore Chloë’s romantic history beyond the spotlight.

On a Monday episode of Watch What Happens Live!, Chloë, at 19, openly acknowledged her relationship with the 17-year-old Brooklyn Beckham. In a candid moment during a game of Plead the Fifth, she shared, “I think the more I don’t make it mysterious, the more people don’t care, so yes, we’re in a relationship.” When asked about Brooklyn’s famous parents, she praised them as great parents, emphasizing their role in raising a “pretty son.”

Social Media Declarations

Brooklyn didn’t shy away from expressing his affection on social media. He shared adorable photos of Chloë, even referring to her as his “bae” in a portrait posted on May 6. These public declarations provided fans with glimpses into their relationship and added fuel to dating speculations that had been circulating since 2014.

From Hanging Out to Teen Choice Awards

The dating rumors initially surfaced in 2014 when Chloë and Brooklyn were spotted hanging out together. Their connection grew stronger as they attended the Teen Choice Awards as dates. Despite their young age, their relationship gained attention, and fans began speculating about the nature of their bond.

Family Dinners and Inseparable Moments

In mid-April, the couple further fueled rumors when they were spotted at dinner with their families, reportedly “inseparable all night.” A source shared with PEOPLE, “They had a massive PDA thing going on. Chloë was stroking Brooklyn’s arm, and at one point, Brooklyn had his head on Chloë’s shoulder. They definitely seemed like a couple.”

Chloë Grace Moretz Love Life

Chloë’s acting career has soared with roles in movies like Kick-Ass, Carrie, and The Equalizer. In her most recent project, a Hulu original movie called Mother Android, premiering on December 17, 2021, Chloë explores the challenges faced by two young adults fleeing a country entangled in a battle against artificial intelligence.

Apart from Mother Android, Chloë is also part of an Amazon Prime series called The Peripheral. Her Instagram showcases six months of dedication to this project, emphasizing her commitment to her career. With over 19.2 million Instagram followers, Chloë’s professional life keeps her engaged, explaining her current single status.

As of now, Chloë appears to be single, dedicating her time and energy to her thriving career. The intensity of her recent projects, including Mother Android and The Peripheral, suggests a busy schedule that leaves little room for a romantic relationship. Despite her single status, Chloë’s Instagram reflects a fulfilling life, featuring camping trips, quality time with her dog, and social outings with friends.

Chloe Grace Moretz Romantic Past

In 2013, Chloë was rumored to be dating Cameron Fuller, son of producer Bradley Fuller. Spotted hanging out in West Hollywood, the media speculated about their relationship, though the rumors were never officially confirmed.

Chloë’s most prominent romance unfolded from 2014 to 2018 with Brooklyn Beckham. Meeting at Paris Fashion Week, their relationship blossomed, marked by joint appearances and interviews. The couple attended SoulCycle classes together and openly discussed each other in the media. Post-breakup rumors linked Chloë to Dylan O’Brien, although nothing was confirmed.

Following her breakup with Brooklyn, Chloë entered a relationship with model Kate Harrison from 2018 to 2019. Kate, known for her Playboy Magazine photoshoot, became part of Chloë’s public romantic history.

Is Chloë Grace Moretz Currently in a Relationship?

As of now, Chloë Grace Moretz is not in a relationship. While she has had public romances in the past, recent information indicates that she is currently single, focusing on her career and personal growth.

Why Did Chloe Grace Moretz Take a Break from Acting?

Chloë Grace Moretz decided to step back from her future film projects due to a realization that she needed to “slow down.” Speaking at the Deauville American Film Festival, she mentioned that her hectic work schedule made her lose sight of why she became an actress in the first place. This break allowed her to reevaluate and prioritize her well-being.

What is Chloe Grace Moretz Best Known For?

Chloë Grace Moretz is widely recognized for her role in the sci-fi thriller series, The Peripheral. Created by Scott B., the series has showcased Moretz’s talent and contributed to her fame in the entertainment industry.
