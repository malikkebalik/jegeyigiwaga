Deadline day transfers: All the done deals in Premier League and across Europe on final day of 2023

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Transfer deadline day is over — meaning clubs will have to wait until January to bolster or trim their squads again.

The 2023 summer window closed for Premier League clubs and sides around Europe on Friday, September 1, though options remain beyond that date for whose who failed to land a new employer.
