Younan Biography: Age, Parents, Siblings

Younan was born on July 6, 1974, in Chicago, Illinois. She holds an American nationality; and has Syrian-Lebanese descent and an Afro-American ethnicity. Her father is from Syria, while her mother is from Beirut, Lebanon.

As for her siblings, she has a younger sister named Bella Youkhana and two brothers named Samuel Younan and Ninos Younan. Her brother Ninos is an Investment Manager and an owner of the Moza Investments.

The Chicago native graduated with a degree in Political Science from the University of Illinois. During her college, she was a part of the Piven Theatre Workshop and was involved in various theatres.

Her Career Height Is Only Getting Higher

After college, Younan started a freelance writing job and wrote about fitness, fashion, and beauty. With a height of 5 feet and 2 inches, the hardworking and attractive lady started working as a fitness model.

Younan Parents Four Kids with Scottie Pippen

Besides all the fame Younan has earned for herself, she is also well known as the ex-wife of NBA superstar Scottie Pippen. Her husband Pippen was previously married to Karen McCollum from 1988 until 1990 and fathered three children with his then-wife. 

However, the NBA player's marriage with McCollum failed to last the distance and culminated in a separation after which he met Younan. Once titled the Hottest Wife of NBA star, Younan and Pippen were married for more than 20 years. The loved-up couple tied the knots in 1997 at her hometown Chicago.

After few years of marriage, the then-couple welcomed their firstborn, a son Scottie Pippen Jr. in 2002. And until 2009, they had already parented four kids, Preston (2004), Justin (2007), and Sophia (2009).

Younan's Husband Filed for Divorce at First

Do all good things have to come to an end? What looked like a perfect family from outside was burning in the insides, and the family's struggles only came to light in 2016 when her husband filed for divorce.

Announcing the news of the separation of the darling couples TMZ reported at that time:

Younan, who is good friends with the Kardashians, moved into fellow single mother Kourtney Kardashian's Calabasas mansion during the split.

She Filed for Divorce after a Short Reconciliation in 2018

Younan and Pippen shared an on and off-again relationship. After filing for divorce in October 2016, they reconciled in June 2017 to consider divorce in August the same year. Throughout reconciliations, she and her husband tried their best to make the marriage work out for their four children.
