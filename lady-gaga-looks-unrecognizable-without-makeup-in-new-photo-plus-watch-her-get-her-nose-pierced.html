Lady Gaga looks unrecognizable without makeup in new photo, plus watch her get her nose pierced

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Lady Gaga is looking different these days in preparation for her ARTPOP album. She seems to be going for a more bare-faced, light-eyebrows look.

Everyone went nuts when she posted the above photo on her Little Monsters social media site. I think the biggest shock factor here is her eyebrows, which appear to be bleached. She usually colors them to be darker than normal. Eyebrows are major players in the face game.

Lady Gaga added a poetic caption to the above photo: “ARTPOP as they pry the single from my bleeding fingers. it’s a scary thing to revisit those things underneath, the pain in your past. but all I found was raw passion. I thought I was destroyed inside. I’m just ready to fight. start the music.”

Gaga’s transformation to her new pop album selling form isn’t done yet! Her makeup artist Tara Savelo posted a YouTube clip of Gaga getting her new nose pierced. This gives new meaning to “Poker Face” (Sorry, I know:)
