With a long list of film and television credits, Gwendoline Christie is one of Hollywood’s most well-known actors. Most famously, Christie, who stands 6 feet 3 inches tall, as the tall warrior Brienne of Tarth in the HBO fantasy drama series Game of Thrones.

When the actress landed the role of Larissa Weems in Tim Burton’s critically acclaimed Netflix series Wednesday, her fame took off even more.

Does Gwendoline Christie Have a Spouse? Her Quiet Romance

In contrast to her career pursuits, the actress has maintained a notoriously low profile when it comes to her romantic life. People are curious about Christie’s dating status in 2023 even though she has a propensity to keep things quiet.

Particularly since Gwendoline starred in a few critically renowned TV shows like Wednesday and Game of Thrones, many people have been curious as to whether the 44-year-old actress is married.

Yes, that is not the case! Gwendoline is single right now and hasn’t disclosed anything about her spouse. So, in 2023, who is Christie dating? In the next headings, we’ll discuss her partner and boyfriend.

Giles Deacon, Gwendoline Christie’s longtime partner, is now her partner.

Christie has not yet tied the knot with anyone, but she and her lover Giles Deacon have been enjoying a blissful relationship as of late. They are both well-known individuals in the entertainment sector.

Although Gwendoline is an actor, her partner Deacon has established a strong reputation for himself in the fashion industry with elaborate, dramatic, and culturally-inspired creations. The well-known British creative director and fashion designer has even created pieces for his partner throughout the years.

Christie with Giles, her partner.

“One of my favorite designers for a very long time is Giles. He possesses two qualities that I find most admirable in artists: a creative mind and meticulous technique.

Even though [his designs] are sculptural, mind-bending, ethereal, and useful, wearing them always makes you feel like a celebrated woman. In some way, their confidence boosts yours. It is quite the ability for a designer to possess. His outfits have individualities.

“I’ve stopped using social media.” It’s not really healthy, in my opinion. It’s better not to know. Furthermore, it is utterly self-indulgent.

“That those people are my pals is a great blessing. I’m fortunate to have them all as wonderful individuals. They work tremendously hard in an extremely challenging sector and are brilliantly talented and intelligent. Almost all of my buddies are artists.

Deacon, Christie’s partner, was smitten with her.

According to some accounts, the two initially crossed paths in 2012. For the designer Giles Deacon, it was love at first sight when they first met. With her, he fell “instantly smitten” in love. In 2018, he told Yahoo! News.

“I was instantly smitten when I met Gwendoline six years ago through a mutual friend.”

He continued,

I felt she was exceptional for a million different reasons. I had never seen anything like the way she carried herself before.

The seasoned designer has since collaborated on other projects as a professional team alongside his partner. It is a “absolute joy to work with professionally, and we’ve dressed her for many occasions, most recently the Emmys,” he once said in an interview with Britain’s Grazia magazine.

Deacon continued, saying that his long-term partner “knows more about film and fashion than I do, and she has her own unique sense of self.” Having that to play with is fantastic.

Star of Game of Thrones Gwendoline Started Dating Giles on New Year’s Eve in 2012

Gwendoline Christie’s longtime partner Deacon and her lover Gwendoline.
