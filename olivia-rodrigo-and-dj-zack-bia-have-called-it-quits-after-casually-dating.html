Olivia Rodrigo and DJ Zack Bia Have Called It Quits After Casually Dating!

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Olivia Rodrigo and Zack Bia have called off their relationship just months after dating. source: Page Six

  • Olivia Rodrigo and DJ Zack Bia have parted ways after a short time of dating.
  • The pair were casually dating and there was nothing serious between them.
  • Rumors of the couple dating started circling around in February when they were spotted making out in public.

Olivia Rodrigo and DJ Zack Bia are no longer together

After a few months of dating, Olivia Rodrigo and Zack Bia have called it quits.

A source who reported their split said:

“Their relationship sort of fizzled. They were casually dating but haven’t spent time together in a while,” the insider reveals. “There were no issues — they both have different work schedules and different friend groups.”

Just two weeks ago, Rodrigo,19, and Bia, 26, were making out while casually dating.

Fans first noticed something was going on between the couple when they were hanging out at a Super Bowl party in February.

After their first meeting, the pair started seeing more of each other but kept the details of their relationship a secret.

That very month, Rodrigo split from her then-boyfriend, Adam Faze, just after less than a year of dating.

It was after an interview when she revealed about moving forward from breakups that fans started suspecting that the two had called it quits.

The singer said at the time:

“Besides cutting off all contact, I think it’s important to not only forgive them but forgive yourself for letting everything happen. That’s what I’ve learned,”

Her romance with the DJ was seemingly confirmed when the pair were pictured getting cozy together in NYC. Bia put his arm around the Brutal artist as they strolled, fueling more rumors of their romance.

The  former couple’s dating history

Olivia was also previously linked to her costar Joshua Bassett whom she met on the set of High School Musical. The pair, however, went separate ways after less than a year of dating.

Zack, for his part, formerly dated Madison Beer on and off for almost a year before finally splitting in March 2019.

He was also reportedly dating Outer Banks star Madelyn Cline earlier this year but the Field Trip record label founder claimed that there was nothing serious between them.

He clarified:

”We are not dating,”

“We hang out all the time. It is one of those things where we really enjoy hanging out and enjoy going to dinner. But we never officially started dating and probably will never officially stop because we are just hanging out. We are enjoying it and not even thinking about what it is.”
