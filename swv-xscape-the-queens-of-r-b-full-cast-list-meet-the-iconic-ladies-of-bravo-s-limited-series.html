'SWV &amp; Xscape: The Queens of R&amp;B' Full Cast List: Meet the iconic ladies of Bravo's limited series

September 2024 · 5 minute read

ATLANTA, GEORGIA: Following the mind-blogging Verzuz performance, Xscape and SWV ladies are back together. Well, this time they will be seen spilling the tea on the limited drama series 'SWV & Xscape: The Queens of R&B'. The ladies will be witnessed showing off their reigniting music careers. 

Bravo's limited series will give us a detailed view of the past, present, and future of Xscape and SWV. The official synopsis claimed, "There is much at stake for these powerhouse women as they navigate different stages in their lives, both personally and professionally." From celebrating the collaboration of the two most hit R&B groups to battling and reconciling the relationships, we will witness these powerhouse women go through a lot of ups and downs. Brace yourself for drama, laughter, and outlandish musical performances on the screen. Keep reading to know more about the cast members appearing in 'SWV & Xscape: The Queens of R&B'

Who are the cast members of 'SWV & Xscape: The Queens of R&B'?

SWV (Sisters With Voices) Members

Cheryl 'Coko' Gamble

Cheryl "Coko" Gamble, 52, is the lead singer of the SWV group. She stepped into the music world and began singing at the age of 3. She rose to fame in 1992 with her hit R&B album 'It's About Time.' The singer has earned 11 Billboard Music Award nominations. She has also bagged nominations for Grammy, American Music Award, Source Award, Children's Choice Award, and BET Best of Video Soul Award. She also released several solo albums. Coko is a staunch LGBTQIA+ ally and an advocate for those suffering from mental health.

Tamara 'Taj' Johnson-George

Tamara "Taj" Johnson-George, 51, is the singer of the SWV group. Taj joined the group in the late 80s and rose to fame in 1992. After SWV officially disbanded in 1998, Taj signed a modeling contract with Ford Modeling Agency. She married Eddie George in 2004 and shares a son Eriq. Moreover, Taj is the stepmother to Jaire. She was also one of the cast members of 'Survivor' Season 18. As of now, Taj is reported to be pursuing a law degree.

Leanne 'Lelee' Lyons

Leanne "Lelee" Lyons, 49, is the singer as well as the founder of the SWV group. Lelee bagged fame at a very early age. By the time she was 17, she has had two children, Margaret and Khiry, and formed a group. In 2019, she began working on her autobiography, 'I regret Day I Lost My Virginity: You Are Not Your Past.' She is currently working on her audiobook 'The Adventures of Sister Hot P***y,' where she will be seen openly talking about her sexual instances. The star is also the mastermind behind an upcoming apparel line 'Cutee in a Hoodie.'

Xscape Members

Kandi Burruss Tucker

Kandi Burruss Tucker, 46, is a singer, songwriter, actor, producer, restauranteur and 'RHOA' star. Kandi formed the group Xscape with high school friends Tameka “Tiny” Harris, LaTocha Scott, and Tamika Scott in 1993. Kandi is married to Todd Tucker and they share four children, Riley, Ace, Blaze, and Kaela. Kandi's group Xscape disbanded in 1999. However, the group came back together in 2017.

Tameka 'Tiny' Harris

Tameka "Tiny" Harris, 47, got famous when she was a young girl. She entered the industry as a model working in commercials. She then joined Xscape and became world-famous. Tiny is a prolific songwriter who has penned down TLC's 'No Scrubs.' Tiny is well known as the better half of TI and the mother of seven children. Tiny's children are stars in making, her son King has recently got himself acquainted with the rap world while her daughter Heiress is a star in making.

Tamika Scott

Tamika Scott, 47, is the sister of LaTocha Scott and a high school friend of Tiny and Kandi. She appeared in the 2009 movie 'There's a Stranger in My House.' Moreover, he stars performed in various of Tyler Perry's stage plays. She is a performer and a cook as well. Culinary prowess has her cookbook released 'Table Set: Cooking with Tamika Scott' released in 2022. She is married to Darnell and is a mother of three daughters, O’Shun, Niyah, and Princess. Her daughter Niyah is also an aspiring rapper like her.

LaTocha Scott-Bivens

LaTocha Scott-Bivens, 49, is a singer, author, and entrepreneur. One-fourth of the prolific R&B quartet band, LaTocha formed the group with her sister Tamika and high school friends. She began her singing career in the church. She was well-known in her hometown for her soulful voice. As she grew up, she was encouraged to pursue a career in the same. LaTocha is also passionate about her health and wellness. She released her cookbook 'LaTocha’s Planted Lifestyle' in 2020 and created a fitness line of 'Made Girl' athleisure wear. She is married to Rocky Bivens and has a son named Jamon Bivens.

'SWV & Xscape: The Queens of R&B' premieres Sunday, March 5, at 9.30 PM ET/PT on Bravo.
