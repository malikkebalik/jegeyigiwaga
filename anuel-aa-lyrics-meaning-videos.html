Overall Meaning

In Anuel AA's song "1942", he explores the complexities of relationships, the influence of money and power, and the truth that alcohol can reveal. Anuel asks his love interest to be real with him and not just a fan of his persona, acknowledging that women come and go but she is special to him. He compares her beauty to Karel and Celian but admits she may not know who they are, showing that his lifestyle involves many women but he values her over them. Anuel reveals he has been with her three times in a week and has conflicting feelings between being good and bad. Despite this, he claims she is his favorite and searches for her in Puerto Rico.


Anuel continues to express his vulnerability towards love, revealing he does not believe in it and that money and power have changed him. While denying that he knows her ex-boyfriend, he promises to be the same person but admits something has changed in him. The chorus reveals he wants to be loved while alive and not just after death, showing his desire for genuine connection. Anuel acknowledges that he has lost his mind and still hasn't found it, but claims the truth always comes out when someone is drunk. The song ends with Anuel expressing his love for his special someone and wanting to take shots of 1942 with her.
