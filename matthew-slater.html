Biography

Matthew Slater was born in California on September 9, 1985. Standout special teams player who was a 5th round pick of the New England Patriots in 2008. He helped the Patriots win the AFC Championship in 2012 and Super Bowl XLIX in 2015. During his time at UCLA, he played both offense and defense and was a first-team All-American in 2007. He developed into an All-Pro player under the tutelage of legendary Patriots coach Bill Belichick. He was a track and football star at Servite High School in Anaheim, California; he set school records in 100 meters and 200-meter competitions, and on the football field, he caught 39 passes for 707 yards and 5 touchdowns his senior year.
